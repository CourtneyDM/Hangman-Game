Welcome to Hangman - Superhero Edition

How well do you know your superheros? Well, you're about to find out! In this game of hangman, you have to guess the alias of a given superhero. Easy enough, right?


Your Objective:

You will be given a hint in the form of a tagline or movie quote, along with 10 chances to guess the alias of the superhero. Each incorrect guess will cost you, so choose wisely.

NOTE - you will not be penalized for guessing the same letter twice.
